The Philosophy of Silence

Abstraction

I try to abstract the world around me into silence. Maybe it's an escape from a spinning, hasty world.

Integration

Music and poetry are two phenomena that can hardly be thought of separately. When I make music, I look for the poetic.

Sound Experience

Sound experience is always the most important parameter in my work. Where the music stops, the word begins again.
